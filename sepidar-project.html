Sepidar Residential Complex

üìç Guilan, Iran

The Sepidar Residential Complex is a 4-hectare fully developed residential site located in Guilan, Iran.  

As the lead architectural designer at Tose'e Shomal Company from October 2018 to November 2019, I spearheaded the design of this upscale complex containing 19 buildings including villas, duplex houses, swimming pool, children's playgrounds, an intricate network of walking trails, and a man-made stream running throughout the site.

Working closely with my team, I took charge of developing the conceptual design, massing studies, and schematics for the residential development.

Bringing my expertise in biophilic design, I guided the integration of innovative wooden structures and habitat rehabilitation to seamlessly incorporate the architecture into the natural landscape.

Using programs such as Lumion, Autodesk 3ds Max, AutoCAD, Revit and Adobe InDesign, I led the optimal design of the island, buildings, and landscapes using biophilic strategies to create a comfortable, nature-integrated living space for the community.

The complex provides amenities like dedicated resting areas, outdoor TVs, and modern parking for each building to fulfill resident needs.

Through leading the architectural vision and directing the technical execution, I played an instrumental role in bringing to life this unique residential community fused with Guilan's beautiful natural setting.
